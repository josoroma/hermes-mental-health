# GAD-7 Interpretation Guidelines

## Score ranges

| Score | Severity | Clinical action |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| 0-4 | Minimal | No treatment indicated |
| 5-9 | Mild | Watchful waiting; repeat GAD-7 at follow-up |
| 10-14 | Moderate | Further evaluation; consider treatment |
| 15-21 | Severe | Active treatment indicated |

## Differential considerations

GAD-7 is a screening tool, not diagnostic. Elevated scores warrant evaluation for:
- Generalized Anxiety Disorder
- Panic Disorder
- Social Anxiety Disorder
- PTSD (consider PCL-5)
- Medical causes of anxiety (thyroid, cardiac, substance-induced)

## Functional impairment

GAD-7 item 8 ("If you checked off any problems, how difficult have these problems
made it for you to do your work, take care of things at home, or get along with
other people?") assesses functional impact and should be documented separately.

## Monitoring

Re-administer at clinically appropriate intervals to track treatment response.
A 5-point change is considered clinically meaningful.
