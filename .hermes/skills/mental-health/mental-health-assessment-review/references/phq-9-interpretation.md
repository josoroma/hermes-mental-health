# PHQ-9 Interpretation Guidelines

## Score ranges and clinical actions

| Score | Severity | Clinical action |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| 0-4 | None/minimal | No treatment indicated |
| 5-9 | Mild | Watchful waiting; repeat PHQ-9 at follow-up |
| 10-14 | Moderate | Treatment plan; counseling, follow-up, and/or pharmacotherapy |
| 15-19 | Moderately severe | Active treatment with pharmacotherapy and/or psychotherapy |
| 20-27 | Severe | Immediate initiation of pharmacotherapy and expedited referral to mental health specialist |

## Item 9 (self-harm)

Item 9 ("Thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way"):
- Score >= 1 → requires further assessment for suicide risk
- Score >= 2 → urgent clinical attention needed
- Always document the response to item 9 separately in clinical notes

## Functional impairment

PHQ-9 item 10 ("How difficult have these problems made it for you to do your work,
take care of things at home, or get along with other people?") is a functional
impairment indicator, not scored in the total but documented separately.

## Monitoring frequency

- Acute phase: every 1-2 weeks
- Continuation phase: every 2-4 weeks
- Maintenance phase: every 4-12 weeks
