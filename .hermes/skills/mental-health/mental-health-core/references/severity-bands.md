# Severity Bands

## Five-band system (PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5)

| Band | Color | Range (PHQ-9) | Clinical meaning |
|------|-------|--------------|------------------|
| None | Green #22c55e | 0-4 | No clinically significant symptoms |
| Mild | Yellow #eab308 | 5-9 | Subthreshold; monitor |
| Moderate | Orange #f97316 | 10-14 | Clinically significant; consider treatment |
| Moderately severe | Dark orange #ea580c | 15-19 | Active treatment indicated |
| Severe | Red #ef4444 | 20-27 | Intensive treatment; assess safety |
| Unscorable | Grey #6b7280 | — | Cannot compute; review data quality |
