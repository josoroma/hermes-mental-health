1. Psychoeducation on the mind-body connection, autonomic arousal, and the stress-symptom cycle

3. Behavioral experiments to test illness-related predictions
4. Interoceptive exposure and symptom tolerance training
5. Graded activity scheduling with pacing to break the boom-bust cycle
6. Attention training to reduce hypervigilance toward bodily cues
7. Relapse prevention planning
