## Reason for Consultation

[Patient] presents with a constellation of mood, anxiety, and somatic complaints that have intensified over the past six to twelve months. The primary concerns reported include:

- Persistent low mood with diminished interest in previously enjoyed activities
- Heightened anxiety manifesting as excessive worry, restlessness, and difficulty controlling ruminative thoughts
- Increased irritability affecting interpersonal relationships at home and in the workplace
- Somatic symptoms including chronic fatigue, muscle tension, and gastrointestinal distress without clear medical etiology
- Sleep disturbance characterized by frequent nocturnal awakenings and non-restorative sleep

The patient reports that symptoms have progressively worsened, impacting occupational performance and social functioning. There is no reported history of imminent self-harm risk at intake, though passive death ideation is acknowledged.
