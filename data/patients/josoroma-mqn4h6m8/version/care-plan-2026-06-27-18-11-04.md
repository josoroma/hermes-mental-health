## Recommended Interventions

- **Modality:** Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT) for Somatic Symptom Disorder, weekly individual sessions, 50 minutes, 12-16 sessions
- **Phase 1 (Sessions 1-3):** Validate somatic experience, psychoeducation on mind-body connection and central sensitization, collaborative biopsychosocial formulation, self-monitoring via daily symptom log
- **Phase 2 (Sessions 4-10):** Cognitive restructuring targeting catastrophic misinterpretation of bodily sensations, behavioral experiments testing illness predictions, attention training and interoceptive exposure, graded activity scheduling with pacing
- **Phase 3 (Sessions 11-16):** Symptom tolerance training, relapse prevention planning, graduated step-down (weekly to biweekly to monthly boosters)
- **Adjunctive as indicated:** Behavioral activation if PHQ-9 10+, exposure-based work if GAD-7 10+, mindfulness (MBSR/MBCT) for arousal regulation, psychiatry referral if moderate-severe depression or anxiety confirmed
- **Medical Coordination:** Formal communication with PCP, single-point-of-contact medical management, coordination updates every 4-6 weeks, conservative diagnostic stewardship
- **Medication:** Psychiatry referral for SNRI evaluation (e.g., duloxetine 30-60 mg/day) if comorbid depression/anxiety confirmed. Avoid benzodiazepines and opioid analgesics.
